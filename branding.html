Branding and Visual Identity

Consistent branding helps audiences recognise and trust Tasmanian Government communication. Each department may have slight variations, but all should follow the central government standards.

The official TasGov Communications site is the source of truth for logos and identity:

  • TasGov Communications Toolbox
  • Government Logos and Style
