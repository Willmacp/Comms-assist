  1. Brief approved by Director or ELT-level lead
  2. Planning tools complete (OASIS, narrative, evaluation method)
  3. Risk and stakeholder scan (e.g. checklist or peer review)
  4. Creative checked for accessibility and branding compliance
  5. Implementation calendar approved
