What is MCOM?

The Modern Communications Operating Model (MCOM) is a framework developed by the UK Government Communication Service (GCS) to help structure and manage communications functions within government. It is used to build high-performing teams and drive better public outcomes through strategic communications.

This version has been adapted to suit the Tasmanian Government context, supporting whole-of-government collaboration, improved standards, and consistent delivery across departments and agencies.

Key Principles

  • All comms teams are strategic, insight-driven and audience-focused
  • Shared capabilities enable efficiency and skill-sharing across government
  • Professional development and evaluation are core parts of the model
  • Modern tools and data should underpin planning and delivery
