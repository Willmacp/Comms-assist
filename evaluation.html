The GCS Evaluation Framework

The UK Government Communication Service (GCS) Evaluation Framework is a six-stage cycle used by departments across the UK and internationally to structure campaign measurement. It aligns well with the OASIS model and gives communicators a clear structure for learning, planning, and improvement.

The six stages are:

  1. Inputs: What resources are you putting in?
  2. Outputs: Who is seeing it and how?
  3. Outtakes: What is being understood or retained?
  4. Outcomes: What has changed as a result?
  5. Impact: What wider effect has this had on society or policy?
  6. Learning: What can we do differnrly now, or in the future?
