Tasmanian Government Context

The Tasmanian Government emphasises a proactive and coordinated approach to crisis and issue management, with key focus areas including:

  • Anticipation and Tracking: Proactively identify and monitor potential issues.
  • Effective Communication: Communicate clearly and promptly to maintain public trust.
  • Coordination: Work across departments and agencies for consistent messaging.
