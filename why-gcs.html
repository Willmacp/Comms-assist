What is GCS?

The UK Government Communication Service (GCS) is the professional body for public sector communicators across the UK. It sets the standard for strategic, insight-driven, and measurable government communication ‚Äî and is widely regarded as a world leader in public sector communications practice.

GCS frameworks

Comms Assist is based on the successful models developed by GCS, including:

  • OASIS ‚Äî a framework for campaign planning
  • MCOM ‚Äî the Modern Communications Operating Model for building high-performing comms functions
  • The Evaluation Cycle ‚Äî six stages to assess communications impact
  • Behaviour change models ‚Äî including COM-B and EAST

These frameworks are simple, adaptable, and designed for use across teams of all sizes ‚Äî from central departments to regional agencies and ALBs.

What has GCS achieved?

The UK model has helped government communicators deliver:

  • Increased citizen trust and engagement
  • More efficient campaign spend through shared tools and templates
  • Stronger outcomes, including during crises (COVID-19, cost of living, public health)
  • Clear evaluation and reporting to show ROI
