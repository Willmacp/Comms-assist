Writing Guidance

Use clear, plain language to remove barriers for readers. For local alignment, refer to:

  • State Growth Inclusive Writing Guide

Checklist for Accessibility

  • Use meaningful headings, lists, and formatting
  • Provide alt text for images
  • Use high contrast colours and readable fonts
  • Ensure documents are screen-reader friendly (e.g. tagged PDFs)
  • Test your communications with diverse users if possible
