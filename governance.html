Governance

The Communications Governance section provides guidance for ensuring your communication activities align with internal processes, approvals, and public sector obligations. Good governance protects your project, your team, and your audiences â€” and it helps make the case for communications being a strategic function.

What to include in your governance process

  • Clear approval steps for campaign stages and messaging
  • Engagement with legal, policy, or ministerial teams when necessary
  • Defined roles and responsibilities, including senior ownership
  • Sign-off from relevant executives, boards, or sponsors
  • Processes for issues management, risks, and lessons learned
