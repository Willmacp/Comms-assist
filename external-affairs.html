External Affairs

External affairs covers how your agency engages with external organisations, such as business, industry, NGOs, unions, community groups and peak bodies. These stakeholders can be critical influencers or delivery partners and are often affected by policy or service delivery decisions.

Comms teams should work closely with policy, engagement and ministerial staff to support the right approach and to avoid duplication.
